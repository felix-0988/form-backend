How It Works

Three steps to working forms

1

Create Your Form

Sign up and create a new form endpoint. We'll give you a unique form ID and submit URL.

POST /api/forms/create
2

Connect Your HTML

Point your form's action attribute to our endpoint. Works with any HTML form, no JavaScript needed.

action=".../api/forms/{id}/submit"
3

Collect Submissions

Submissions flow into your dashboard instantly. Get email notifications and view analytics.

GET /dashboard/{id}
